About Me! I am Kin Ka Tse and I programmed this in a week! Technologies include TensorFlow and Canvas.
How To Use! Enable your camera (If you have zoom or some other application, you will need to turn the camera off there for this to work). The face detection may take a few seconds to load up so please give it a moment. Once you see your video feed, you can play the game by pressing start. The bombs will do damage and take away score. The red circle is an apple you can eat to heal health, however you can only eat it by physically opening your mouth. The yellow circle is coins to increase your score. Play until you get a high score! The way this works is by grabbing the facial points and using that as the hurtbox to your character. So now you have to physically move your face around to avoid and reach certain obstacles.
