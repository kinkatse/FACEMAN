Hello! Welcome to FACE-MAN

This is a face detection game which uses your video feed to display obstacles. Your face is your hurtbox so your objective is to avoid all obstacles while collecting as many coins as possible! Once the video feed is displayed, you can start the game. You can also apply a filter while playing!

There are 4 entities to be aware of in this game

Below is a BOMB entity. Try to avoid getting hit! Manuever around the bombs and don't let it touch your face! If the health bar reaches 0, that is game over!

Below is an APPLE entity. This will heal you! To eat an apple, well, you need to open your mouth! Then move your mouth to the apple and you will heal!

Below is a COIN entity. This will add to your score. Simply touch the coin with your face to get points! Note: Taking damage will decrease your score!

Below is an GHOST entity. These are sometimes unavoidable obstacles since they are big and slow, and do a lot of damage. The only way to avoid the ghost is to squint your eyes so the ghost thinks you're asleep and passes over you
